# Doli App 🚀

> A smart, color-coded productivity and Pomodoro assistant built with React and Firebase.

## 🎯 Overview
Staying focused in a hectic schedule just got a lot easier! I am thrilled to share **Doli**, a productivity assistant I developed specifically for students, developers, and anyone looking to build sustainable study habits.

Doli is more than just a timer; it’s a comprehensive assistant that analyzes your data, provides personalized statistics, and helps you maintain your peak focus.

## 🌟 Key Features
* 🎯 **Focus & Break Modes:** Manage your deep work sessions and recovery time with precision.
* 🔔 **Background Operation & Notifications:** Doli keeps working even when you're on another tab. Receive instant browser notifications and audio alerts the moment your session ends.
* 🔐 **Fast & Secure Login:** Powered by **Firebase**, you can sign in instantly with your Google account and access your data securely across all devices.
* 📊 **Personalized Analytics:** Track your daily work and break patterns on a weekly basis. Discover which subjects you’ve dedicated the most time to with smart summary cards.
* 🎨 **Color-Coded Tracking:** Personalize your workspace! If you assign "Blue" to your Database course, all your stats and charts will reflect that color for intuitive tracking.
* 📱 **Fully Responsive:** Experience a seamless UI whether you are on your laptop, tablet, or smartphone.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack
- **Frontend:** React, JavaScript (ES6+), HTML5, CSS3
- **Backend/Database:** Firebase (Auth & Firestore)
- **Deployment:** Netlify
